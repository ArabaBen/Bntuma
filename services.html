Our Services & Pricing

Bespoke Designing

Custom garments made to your unique fit and style.

Bespoke Designing

Our bespoke design service provides garments made specifically for your body, style, and occasion. Each piece is crafted by hand with careful measurements, quality fabrics, and finishing details that reflect your individuality.

We believe every body deserves well-fitting, expressive clothing. Whether you need custom suiting, dresses, or traditional attire, we guide you from concept to creation. Bespoke services come with a fast two-week turnaround time. While we provide general price ranges, each piece is uniquely priced based on fabric choice and construction complexity. If the final project cost is lower than estimated, we issue an overcharge refund after completion. And yes, you can bring your own fabric!
