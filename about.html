About Us

BNTUMA is a collective founded by Araba B.O. Graham, a Ghanaian-American designer. Passionate about creating accessible and socially responsible garment and design through innovations, BNTUMA was born. Clothes have no gender, every body deserves good fitting clothes that are comfortable, stylish and made just for them. I believe in the idea of deconstructing and decolonizing how we dress everyday, from mundane chores outfits to special events like weddings and galas. What are we assimilating towards? Are we dimming ourselves? Our light? Our identity? What do we want the world to know about us, who we are, without even saying what or who we are? What we wear in a way speaks to who we are. So why not make that statement be as true, authenticity unwavering as possible. BNTUMA is very culture focused. To know us and our founder is to understand Ghanaian  culture. Coming from a place where going to your local family seamstresses and tailors to get casual and occasional garments made was a way of life has shaped BNTUMA as a business structure and design philosophy. Sustainable, foster creativity &s community. There are intentions behind every commissioned garment. From textile sourcing which is done locally, to purpose and functionality and accessibility of garments for every body. We all deserve clothing that honors our individuality, uplifts our heritage, and celebrates the craft of making. We hope you start and continue to be colorful, be bold, be you Everyday, every occasion. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. I invite you to explore my work, immerse yourself in my designs, and envision the possibilities we can create together. Let's embark on a journey that inspire, connect, and leave a lasting impact.
